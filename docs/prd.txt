Smart Bharat – Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Core Vision
Smart Bharat is a GenAI-powered Civic Companion that simplifies citizen-government interactions through personalized assistance, intelligent recommendations, and a unified digital experience.
1. AI Civic Companion
A conversational AI assistant serving as the primary interface for citizens to access government services.Capabilities:• Natural language conversations• Government service guidance• Context-aware responses• Follow-up conversations• Personalized recommendations• Voice interaction• Multilingual supportExample: 'How do I renew my passport?' The AI explains the process, required documents, fees, timelines, and directs the user to the appropriate service.
2. Smart Government Service Discovery
Helps users discover government services based on their profile instead of searching multiple websites.Features:• Personalized service recommendations• Category-wise exploration• Trending government services• Recently launched schemes• Frequently used services• Nearby government offices
3. AI Scheme Eligibility Engine
Automatically identifies government schemes a citizen may be eligible for.AI analyzes:• Age• Occupation• Income• State• Education• Family details (with permission)Output:• Eligible schemes• Benefits• Required documents• Application deadline• Apply now option
4. Document Intelligence Assistant
Makes government documentation simple and understandable.Features:• Required document checklist• AI explanation of each document• Missing document detection• Document upload• Document verification• AI document summarization
5. AI Form Filling Assistant
Simplifies lengthy government forms.Features:• Smart auto-fill• Field explanations• Error detection• Missing information alerts• Application preview• Submission readiness score
6. Civic Issue Reporting
Allows citizens to report public issues using AI.Supported Issues:Potholes, garbage, water leakage, broken street lights, illegal parking, drainage issues, road damage, public sanitation.Features:• Photo upload• Voice reporting• GPS location detection• AI issue categorization• Priority prediction• Department assignment
7. Complaint Tracking Dashboard
Tracks complaints in real time.Status Flow:Reported → Verified → Assigned → In Progress → ResolvedFeatures:• Timeline• Assigned department• Resolution estimate• Notifications• Citizen feedback
8. Smart Bharat Dashboard
A personalized home screen based on the user's profile.Includes:• Recommended services• Active applications• Complaint status• Upcoming deadlines• Saved documents• AI suggestions• Government notifications
9. Citizen Digital Locker
Secure storage for important government documents.Store:Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving Licence, Certificates, Tax documents, Property documents.Features:• Secure cloud storage• AI document organization• Quick retrieval• Secure sharing
10. Personalized Notifications
Keeps citizens informed without overwhelming them.Notifications:Application updates, complaint updates, eligible schemes, deadline reminders, document expiry alerts, government announcements.
11. AI Knowledge Simplifier
Converts complex government language into simple explanations.Features:• Plain-language summaries• FAQ generation• Step-by-step guidance• Policy explanation• Document summarization
12. Multilingual Support
Makes government services accessible to every citizen.Features:• Multiple Indian languages• Voice translation• Regional language interface• AI language switching
13. Voice-First Accessibility
Designed for elderly users, rural citizens, and differently-abled individuals.Features:• Voice commands• Text-to-speech• Speech-to-text• Large text mode• High contrast mode• Screen reader compatibility
14. Smart Civic Map
Interactive map for government and civic services.Displays:Nearby government offices, hospitals, police stations, service centers, complaint hotspots, public utilities.
15. Emergency Assistance Hub
Provides immediate access to emergency services.Services:Police, Ambulance, Fire Department, Women Helpline, Disaster Management, Child Helpline.AI Features:Emergency intent detection, location sharing, one-tap assistance.
16. Citizen Profile & Personalization
Creates personalized civic experiences.Profiles:Student, Farmer, Working Professional, Senior Citizen, Business Owner, Homemaker, Job Seeker, Differently Abled Citizen.
17. AI Insights & Recommendations
Proactively recommends useful government services and actions.Examples:Scholarships, pensions, tax reminders, license renewal alerts, health schemes, skill development programs.
18. Application & Service Tracker
Unified dashboard to monitor government interactions.Track:Passport applications, driving licences, certificates, scheme applications, complaints, service requests.
19. Feedback & Citizen Engagement
Allows citizens to improve public services.Features:• Rate services• Department feedback• Resolution satisfaction• Suggest improvements• Community participation
20. Admin & Government Dashboard
Dashboard for government departments to manage citizen requests.Features:• Complaint management• Citizen analytics• Department performance• AI issue prioritization• Service monitoring• Resolution tracking• Heatmaps• Reports
AI Features – Unique Selling Points
• Conversational AI – Understands natural language and answers citizen queries.• Context Memory – Remembers previous interactions with permission.• Intent Detection – Understands actual citizen needs.• Personalized Recommendations – Suggests relevant schemes and services.• Document Intelligence – Explains, verifies, and summarizes documents.• Predictive Assistance – Reminds users about renewals and deadlines.• Multilingual AI – Supports Indian languages.• Voice AI – Enables hands-free interaction.• Smart Search – Retrieves government information instantly.• Explainable AI – Explains recommendations and eligibility decisions.
